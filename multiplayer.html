Welcome to 2-player Dice Game!

How to Play

  1. Roll the dice! Try to score 20 points.
  2. Players take it in turns to roll. You can roll as much as you want on your turn.
  3. If you roll a 1, your score resets to 0 and the turn passes to the other player.
  4. If you feel like your luck is running out, try holding your score to pass your go.
